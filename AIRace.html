The AI Race

 

 

What is The AI race?

The AI race is the global competition between countries trying to advance their AI systems and lead the race to create the most advanced systems of Artificial Intelligence. However, it's not all about innovation. It's about gaining economic power, technological leadership and having influence over global standards.

 

Currently, the US is leading the AI race given that it is home to top AI companies such as OpenAI, Google DeepMind, Microsoft, Meta, and NVIDIA. These companies have lots of resources to invest to advance in AI which is also a reason for them being the top.
