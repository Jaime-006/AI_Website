What is The AI race?

The AI race is the global competition between countries trying to advance their AI systems and lead the race to create the most advanced systems of Artificial Intelligence. However, it's not all about innovation. It's about gaining economic power, technological leadership and having influence over global standards.

 

Who is leading?

Currently, the US is leading the AI race given that it is home to top AI companies. These companies have lots of resources to invest to advance in AI which is also a reason for them being the top. Also, the US has more funds to spend compared to other countries which also influences their position. China comes in at second but something to note is that china is a lot more welcoming in the concept of AI. They have smart cities that have ai facial recognition to help with fiding people if they are missing. They even have a AI that track polution, noise, and population flows. They are merging AI with day to day expiriences and taking a integration approach instead of puring the complete focus on advancement as the US is doing. It is a real competator and we can expect a major spike something in the next few years for china.
