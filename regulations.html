What are regulations for AI and why are they needed?

Regulations for AI are laws, policies, and guidelines designed to govern how artificial intelligence systems are developed, used, and monitored. These regulations are put into place in order to ensure safety, tranceperancy and boundaries when it comes to the user's information.
